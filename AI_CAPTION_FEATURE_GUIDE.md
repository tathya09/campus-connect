# AI Caption Feature Guide

## Where is the AI Feature?

The AI Caption Suggestion feature is located in the **Create Post** dialog.

## How to Access It

1. Click on the **"Create"** button in the left sidebar
2. Upload an image by clicking "Select from computer"
3. Once the image is uploaded, the AI feature automatically appears below the image preview

## What Does It Do?

The AI Caption Generator creates 4 smart, contextual caption suggestions for your post:

### Features:

- **Time-Aware Suggestions**: Generates different captions based on the time of day
  - Morning (5 AM - 12 PM): Morning-themed captions
  - Afternoon (12 PM - 5 PM): Afternoon-themed captions
  - Evening (5 PM - 5 AM): Evening/night-themed captions

- **Variety of Styles**:
  - Casual and friendly
  - Inspirational
  - Descriptive
  - Fun with emojis

- **One-Click Apply**: Click any suggestion to instantly use it as your caption

- **Regenerate**: Don't like the suggestions? Click "Regenerate" for 4 new ones

## How It Works (Technical)

The AI feature uses:

- Client-side JavaScript to generate captions
- Time-based logic to determine appropriate caption style
- Random selection from curated caption templates
- No external API calls (completely free and instant)
